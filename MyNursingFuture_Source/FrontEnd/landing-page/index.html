MyNursingFuture.com.au allows nurses and future nurses to explore the world of primary health care. Find...

  • practical tools to help you understand what makes primary health care unique
  • profiles of various primary health care sectors and the skillsets you need to work in them
  • short video snapshots of a day in the life of a nurse working in refugee health, in a prison, in general practice, and more
  • visual career pathways with descriptions of job roles and responsibilities
  • and if you’re convinced, we have job listings and contacts for next steps

You can measure your current level of nursing practice and learn how to actively plan your career progression with a self-reflection activity and tailored feedback report.

Employers, managers and scholars, you’ll find MyNursingFuture.com.au useful too.
